    Quick explaination

    The game

    The game is based on spreading pheromones in a hex grid.

    • Spread your pheromones to expand your territory.
    • New structures can only be built on tiles that has your pheromones on them.
    • Structures take damage if on tiles without their owners pheromone.

    Be the only player left to win.

    Tile types/Structures

    Resource tiles

    These tiles pulse a blue pheromone with regular intervals.

    Home tile

    The starting tile for each player. Spread pheromones and provides the player with a trickle of income.

    Siphon tile

    Build this to siphon parts of the resource pheromone on the tiles this is built.

    Relay tile

    Spreads your pheromone to expand your territory.

    Wall tile

    Stops pheromone from spreading to this tile. Affects both resource and player pheromone.
